# Audience: Casual (Secondary)

## Who They Are

Followers who don't need Solvea right now but are interested in AI, business automation, and SMB growth. Future customers or referrers.

## Demographics

- Aspiring entrepreneurs, tech enthusiasts, AI curious professionals, marketers
- Pre-launch or working at a company (not yet their own SMB)
- High tech comfort. Age 22-40

## Why They Follow Us

- AI curiosity — real-world applications, not hype
- Business insights — our SMB data teaches them
- Inspiration — "$30 replacing $5K" stories are compelling
- Future need — planning to start a business

## How They Help Us Grow

- Shares/retweets amplify content
- Referrals ("my uncle runs a medspa, he needs this")
- Social proof (high engagement)
- Future conversion (10-20% start a business within 2 years)

## Content Balance

- 70% for [[builders]] (direct conversion)
- 30% for casual (growth + awareness)
