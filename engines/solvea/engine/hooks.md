# Hooks — Opening Line Formulas

Hooks decide 80% of content performance. For Solvea, hooks are powered by pain (missed calls, lost revenue) and curiosity (how one business transformed).

## Hook Categories

### 1. Pain Amplifier
- "you're losing $__/month to missed calls and don't even know it"
- "every voicemail you get is a customer choosing your competitor instead"
- "your receptionist goes home at 5pm. your customers don't"
- "41% of your calls come after hours. who's answering them?"
- "the average service business misses 34% of inbound calls. what's your number?"

### 2. Shocking Stat
- "we analyzed 200+ service businesses. 58% of their day, they're unreachable"
- "one missed call from a medspa client = $350 in lost revenue"
- "a plumber in austin missed 23 calls last week. he had no idea"
- "the #1 time customers call isn't 9-5. it's 6:30-8:30pm"
- "$4,200/month. that's what the average service business loses to unanswered calls"

### 3. Before/After Contrast
- "6 months ago this medspa was missing 40% of calls. today: 97% answered, $14K recovered"
- "they were paying $5,000/month for an answering service. now they pay $30"
- "before: 'sorry, we're closed.' after: 'I'd be happy to book that for you.'"

### 4. Curiosity Gap
- "i watched a dentist discover he'd been losing $18K/month. his face said everything"
- "there's a reason one medspa books 3x more clients than the one next door. it's not their marketing"

### 5. Contrarian / Hot Take
- "hot take: voicemail is the worst customer experience invention ever created"
- "hiring more staff isn't the answer. answering every call is. those aren't the same thing"
- "the answering service industry is a $5B scam. here's why"
- "you don't need a better website. you need to answer the phone"

### 6. Direct Challenge
- "if you run a service business and still use voicemail, we need to talk"
- "you spent $5K on google ads this month. how many of those leads went to voicemail?"

### 7. Result-First
- "97% call answer rate. 30% labor cost reduction. 3 minutes to set up. here's how:"
- "$14K in recovered revenue in 30 days. one AI tool. one medspa."

## Rules

- Always include at least ONE specific number
- Never start with "I" on X
- LinkedIn hooks must earn the "see more" click
- YouTube hooks must work in 5 seconds
- Update this file weekly with winning hooks
