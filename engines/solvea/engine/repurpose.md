# Repurpose Engine — 1 Topic → 10 Platform-Native Posts

One idea goes in, platform-native posts come out. Each one RETHINKS the topic — not reformatted, completely rethought.

## The Repurposing Chain

```
TOPIC IDEA
    │
    ├─→ X: contrarian thread OR pain-point singles
    ├─→ LinkedIn: personal narrative + business lesson
    ├─→ YouTube: tutorial or deep-dive (8-12 min)
    ├─→ Instagram: 7-10 slide carousel
    ├─→ TikTok: 30-60s raw screen recording
    ├─→ Threads: conversational hot take
    ├─→ Facebook: community question
    └─→ Newsletter: weekly intelligence briefing
```

## Rethinking Example

**Topic: "The hidden cost of missed calls"**

- **X** → CONTRARIAN: "voicemail is dead. here's proof"
- **LinkedIn** → STORY: "Last week a dentist told me something that stuck..."
- **YouTube** → TUTORIAL: "How to set up an AI receptionist in 3 minutes"
- **Instagram** → VISUAL: Slide 1: "You're Missing 34% of Calls" → steps
- **TikTok** → DEMO: screen recording of AI answering a real call
- **Threads** → OPINION: "the businesses winning aren't the ones with better marketing. they answer the phone"
- **Facebook** → COMMUNITY: "how do you handle after-hours calls?"
- **Newsletter** → DEEP-DIVE: the full data behind missed calls + the $30 fix

## Production Workflow

1. **Pick topic** from calendar or current events
2. **Define core insight** (one sentence all platforms riff on)
3. **Run through chain** with AI agent
4. **Review**: each post sounds native? hooks strong? data accurate?
5. **Schedule** per [[scheduling]] — stagger across the week

## Rules

- ONE topic per week gets full repurpose treatment
- Every customer story = automatic repurpose candidate
- Every support question asked 3+ times = content topic
- Quality check: would this be useful to someone who doesn't know Solvea?
