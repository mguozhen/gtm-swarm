# Facebook — Solvea Content Playbook

## Platform Rules

- **Format:** Longer text posts, discussion-style, community groups + page
- **Posting frequency:** 3-4x/week
- **Best times:** 9am, 1pm EST
- **Algorithm:** Comments and shares

## Two Strategies

### Groups (primary — highest ROI)
**Target groups:** Local business owner groups, industry-specific (medspa owners, HVAC contractors, dental practice management)

**10:1 Rule:** 10 helpful comments before mentioning product.

### Page (secondary)
Share customer stories, YouTube videos, educational content.

## Content Mix

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Community question | 1x/week | Start discussions |
| Customer story (anonymized) | 1x/week | Social proof |
| Practical tip | 1x/week | Genuine value |
| Poll | 1x/2 weeks | Easy engagement |

## Example Post

```
Quick question for business owners here —

How are you handling after-hours calls?

We just looked at our call data and realized 41% of our
inbound calls come after 5pm. Kinda shocked by that number.

Voicemail? Answering service? Just accept the missed calls?
What's working for you?
```

## What NOT to Do

- NEVER drop a product link as first post in a group
- No corporate-speak
- No fake questions leading to your tool
