# Newsletter — Solvea Content Playbook

## Platform Rules

- **Format:** 1,500-word weekly deep dive
- **Frequency:** 1x/week (Thursday 8am EST)
- **Goal:** Build trusted relationship, drive trials, cross-promote YouTube

## Content Mix (Monthly Rotation)

| Week | Theme | Purpose |
|------|-------|---------|
| Week 1 | Data Deep-Dive | Original insight from customer data |
| Week 2 | Customer Story | Detailed case study with full numbers |
| Week 3 | Industry Insight | Trend in customer communication / AI |
| Week 4 | Tactical Guide | Step-by-step "do this today" content |

## Email Structure

```
SUBJECT LINE: specific, curiosity-driven, under 50 chars
PREVIEW TEXT: complement the subject

OPENING (2-3 sentences): personal hook
MAIN SECTION (800-1000 words): data + story + insight
TAKEAWAY (2-3 bullets): actionable today
CTA: try Solvea free / watch YouTube / reply
SIGN-OFF: casual, first name
```

## Subject Line Formulas

- "The $4,200/month leak nobody checks"
- "What 200 businesses taught us about missed calls"
- "The 6:30pm problem"
- "One medspa. $14K recovered. 3 minutes."
- "Voicemail is dead (here's the data)"

## Example Email

```
Subject: The 6:30pm problem
Preview: When your highest-value customers actually call

Last month we looked at call data from 200+ service businesses.

The single highest-volume call window is 6:30-8:30pm.
After hours. After the receptionist has gone home.

41% of all inbound calls arrive outside business hours.

The after-hours caller is your best customer — they've been
thinking about it all day and finally have a quiet moment.

And they get your voicemail.

73% of after-hours callers who reach voicemail do NOT leave
a message. They just call the next result.

3 things you can do this week:

1. Pull your call logs. Count after-hours calls.
2. Multiply missed calls × average customer value.
3. Test being reachable after hours for one month.

Hit reply and tell me: what's your after-hours situation like?

Talk soon,
[Name]
```

## What NOT to Do

- No multiple CTAs, no promotional tone, no more than 1x/week
