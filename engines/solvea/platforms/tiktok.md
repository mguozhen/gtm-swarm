# TikTok — Solvea Content Playbook

## Platform Rules

- **Format:** 30-60s vertical video, raw screen recordings or talking head
- **Posting frequency:** 3-5x/week
- **Best times:** 7-9pm EST weekdays, 11am-1pm weekends
- **Algorithm:** Watch-through rate is king. Hook in first 2 seconds

## Content Mix

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Live demo / screen recording | 2x/week | "Watch this AI answer a real call" |
| Shocking stat + quick explainer | 1x/week | Stop scroll with a number |
| Before/after story | 1x/week | "This medspa was missing 40% of calls. Then..." |
| Trend-jack / stitch | 1x/week | React to trending business content |
| Day-in-the-life | 1x/2 weeks | "Running 3 businesses with one AI receptionist" |

## Video Structure

```
[HOOK: 2 seconds] — show result or bold claim (text overlay)
[CONTEXT: 5 seconds] — why this matters
[THE HOW: 30-40 seconds] — demo, walkthrough, proof
[RESULT: 5 seconds] — the payoff
[CTA: 3 seconds] — "follow for more" or "link in bio"
```

## Hook Patterns

- "watch this AI answer a real customer call"
- "this $30 tool replaced my entire front desk"
- "POV: you never miss a customer call again"
- "the reason your competitor books more clients than you"
- "3 minutes. that's all it took"

## Content Formats

### 1. The Live Demo (highest performer)
Record screen. Call Solvea number. AI answers. Show booking confirmation. Raw is better.

### 2. The Stat Bomb
Text overlay with shocking number. Voiceover. Under 30 seconds.

### 3. The Speed Setup
Screen record Solvea setup sped up to 60s. Show timer. "3 minutes. That's it."

### 4. The Split Screen Before/After
Left: phone ringing, voicemail. Right: AI answering, booking confirmed.

## What NOT to Do

- No intros. Start with hook IMMEDIATELY
- No stock footage
- No polished corporate videos
- No hard selling — demonstrate, don't pitch
- Text overlays mandatory (80%+ watch without sound)

## Cross-Posting

- Every TikTok → YouTube Shorts → Instagram Reels
- Remove watermark before cross-posting
