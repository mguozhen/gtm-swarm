# Audience: Casual (Secondary)

## Who They Are

E-commerce curious followers who aren't active Amazon sellers yet but are interested in data, AI, consumer insights, and starting an online business. Future customers or referrers.

## Demographics

- **Role:** Aspiring sellers, marketing professionals, data enthusiasts, business students, agency people
- **Business stage:** Pre-launch, side hustle research, or working in e-commerce at a company
- **Tech comfort:** High. They follow AI/data trends
- **Age:** 22-40

## Why They Follow Us

1. **Data curiosity.** "What do 2 billion reviews tell us about what people actually want?" is inherently fascinating
2. **Business insights.** Our analyses teach them about consumer behavior and product markets
3. **Inspiration.** "He found a $500K product idea in 60 seconds" makes them want to start selling
4. **Future need.** They're planning to launch and bookmarking tools

## Content That Resonates

- Surprising data reveals about everyday products (yoga mats, garlic presses, wireless earbuds)
- "What customers really think about [popular product]" — consumer behavior stories
- Hot takes about the e-commerce industry
- Behind-the-scenes of product research methodology
- Review horror stories and funny review compilations

## How They Help Us Grow

- **Shares.** Data content is inherently shareable — "did you know that 34% of..." gets forwarded
- **Referrals.** "My friend sells on Amazon, he needs this" — happens constantly
- **Social proof.** High engagement makes us credible to serious sellers
- **Future conversion.** 15-25% will start selling within 2 years

## Content Balance

- 70% for [[builders]] (direct conversion)
- 30% for casual (growth + awareness)
- Overlap: data reveals with product angles serve both
