# Format Templates — Locked Output Scaffolds

> **Rigid templates beat creative freedom.** Alinea Invest runs 1M+ users on 2 fixed video formats. Their moat is creative infrastructure, not capital. We mirror the discipline: each agent has 1-2 locked output scaffolds + 5 fill-in hook templates. Anything outside these = reviewer auto-reject.

This file is the **format law**. The Builder's job is to keep templates current. The Agent's job is to fill them. The Reviewer's job is to reject anything off-template — no exceptions, no taste calls.

---

## Why locked formats

| Soft prompt ("be contrarian") | Locked template ("`POV: {N} {behavior} + {twist}`") |
|---|---|
| Output varies wildly | Output converges, A/B becomes legible |
| Reviewer judges taste | Reviewer checks compliance |
| Wins don't replicate | Wins replicate verbatim across personas |
| Builder hand-holds every draft | Builder owns the template, Agent self-serves |

When a template starts producing losers, the **template gets rewritten**, not the individual drafts.

## Agent 06 — Reddit

### Scaffold A: Data Forensics Post

```
{Hook line — single sentence, ends with a colon or question mark}

I {action verb} {N} {data-source}. Here's what nobody talks about:

{Finding 1 — 2-3 sentences. One specific number.}

{Finding 2 — 2-3 sentences. One named brand/ASIN.}

{Finding 3 — the counter-intuitive one. 2-3 sentences.}

{Method line — 1 sentence: how I did it / tool used.}

{Open question to community — single line ending with "?"}
```

- Word count: 220-380
- Paragraph rhythm: short / medium / medium / medium / short / short
- Last line MUST be a question (triggers comments → algo boost)

### Scaffold B: Vendor-Renewal Checklist Post

```
{Hook — frames a deadline/decision the reader has}

If you're {about to renew / about to launch / about to switch} {category}, here are the {N} questions that tell you in 15 minutes whether the vendor is bluffing:

1. **{Question 1}** — {what a passing answer looks like}
2. **{Question 2}** — {what a passing answer looks like}
...
{N}. **{Question N}** — {what a passing answer looks like}

{One paragraph synthesis — why these matter together.}

{Disclosure line: which side I'm on / what I built / what I sell.}
```

- Word count: 280-450
- N = 5-7 questions
- Disclosure at the END (not the top) earns trust + survives r/marketing self-promo filters

### 06-reddit hook fill-ins (use ONE per draft)

1. `I {analyzed/scraped/pulled} {N specific} {data-source}. The #{n} thing nobody talks about:`
2. `Hot take: {industry-standard practice} is the most {negative-adjective} metric in {category}. Here's why.`
3. `Your {category} {vendor/competitor/tool}'s {data-point} is a goldmine. Here's what's inside.`
4. `Spent {N} {hours/dollars/months} testing {claim}. Here's what broke.`
5. `If you're {about to do X}, here are the {N} questions that save you ${$amount}.`

## Agent 03 — Blog (Official Website)

### Scaffold A: Diagnostic Walkthrough

```
# {Outcome-promise headline — ≤ 70 chars, contains 1 specific number}

{Lede — 2-3 sentences. The reader's situation. End on the pain.}

## The 30-second version
{1-paragraph TL;DR. Includes one named brand or ASIN. No marketing fluff.}

## Why this matters now
{2-3 paragraphs. ONE recent trigger event (e.g. "Q4 board prep", "Prime Day post-mortem", a regulatory shift, a CIA-data-sourced market event from this month).}

## The {N}-step method
1. **{Step 1 verb}** — {2-3 sentences with one screenshot / data table.}
2. **{Step 2 verb}** — {ditto}
...
{N}. **{Step N verb}** — {ditto}

## Worked example: {real ASIN or category}
{300-500 words walking through the method on one concrete case. Numbers, screenshots, an actionable finding.}

## What this replaces
{Bullet list — manual process, vendor X, tool Y. Be specific.}

## Get started
{1-paragraph call to the free tool. Link goes to a SPECIFIC free tool URL, not a paywall.}
```

- Word count: 1200-2200
- Hero image required (alt text in frontmatter)
- Worked example must use a real ASIN / brand the reader can verify
- N = 4-6 steps (3 is too thin, 7+ is too much friction)

### Scaffold B: Contrarian Frame Reset

```
# {Counter-intuitive claim}

> Pull quote — 1 sentence that distills the whole post.

## What everyone says
{2-3 paragraphs. The conventional wisdom. Quote a competitor or category authority.}

## Why it's wrong (or insufficient)
{3-4 paragraphs. ONE specific failure mode. Include data.}

## What actually works
{4-6 paragraphs. The new frame. Walk through it on a worked example.}

## The 5-minute version
{Numbered checklist the reader can apply Monday morning.}
```

- Word count: 1000-1800
- Must cite a published competitor article / case study by name (proves we did the work)
- One pull-quote per H2

### 03-blog hook fill-ins (use the headline)

1. `The {N}-minute {workflow}: how to {outcome} using {differentiator} instead of {status-quo}`
2. `The {N}-{star/score} layer: the most {adjective} {data-source} in your {category} that no one on your team is reading`
3. `Why {incumbent} can't answer your {role}'s {category} question — and what the {structural} gap actually looks like`
4. `How to build a {event} post-mortem that {outcome} before {trigger}`
5. `The {category} ceiling: what happens when your {metric A} are green but {metric B} drops {N}% in 30 days`

## Agent 07 — Social Media (X / LinkedIn / GitHub)

### Scaffold A: X Thread

```
1/ {Hook tweet — ≤ 280 chars, one specific number, NO "I" start.}

2/ {Setup — 2-3 sentences. The reader's situation.}

3/ {Insight 1 — 1 number, 1 named brand}

4/ {Insight 2 — counterintuitive finding}

5/ {Insight 3 — the proof or method}

6/ {Synthesis — what this means}

7/ {CTA — link in next tweet (not in this one)}
```

- Tweet count: 5-7 (anything longer loses 80% of readers)
- Hook tweet uses one of the hook fill-ins
- CTA goes in a REPLY to your own thread, not the thread itself (algo penalty for external links in main thread)

### Scaffold B: LinkedIn Long-Form

```
{Hook line — ≤ 210 chars before the "see more" break. One number.}

{The story — 2-3 short paragraphs. Setup → tension → reveal.}

{The lesson — 1 paragraph, 1 numbered list of 3-5 points.}

{The synthesis — 1 paragraph.}

{The ask — 1 line, ends with a question to drive comments.}

—
{P.S. line with the link / free tool / what to do next.}
```

- Total length: 1500-2500 chars
- Must use 1+ line breaks every 2 sentences (no wall of text)
- Question at the end is REQUIRED (drives comment-rate, which drives reach)

### 07-social hook fill-ins

1. `{Specific number} {entity} did {action}. {Counter-intuitive result}.`
2. `Stop {industry-default behavior}. Start {contrarian alternative}. Here's why:`
3. `LinkedIn carousel: {Incumbent A} vs. {Solution B} side-by-side — what a ${$amount}/year contract misses on {specific use case}`
4. `Thread: the #{1} {pain-point} hiding inside {N} top-{category} on {platform} right now — and which {brand} is winning by ignoring it`
5. `POV: you're a {role} and your {boss/board} just asked "{specific question}". You have 30 minutes. Here's what to pull.`

## Agent 05 — Video (TikTok / YouTube Shorts)

### Scaffold A: Loop-Engineered Short (5-7s)

```
[FRAME 1 — 0.0s] {Hook text overlay, ≤ 8 words, big white sans-serif on muted background}
[FRAME 2 — 1.5s] {Reveal text overlay — answers the hook}
[FRAME 3 — 3.0s] {Data point or screenshot of VOC AI tool — 1 number on screen}
[FRAME 4 — 4.5s] {Punchline / CTA cue}
[FRAME 5 — 6.0s] {Identical to FRAME 1 — closes the loop seamlessly}

AUDIO: {Trending audio cut to 5-7s exactly. Beats align to frame transitions.}
```

- **Hard rule**: first and last frame must be visually identical (within 50ms). This is the Alinea moat — TikTok measures "rewatch" as a completion >100% signal.
- No founder face, no talking-head voiceover. Faceless aesthetic = scalable + replicable across personas.
- Trending audio is mandatory; if no trending audio fits the topic this week, the topic waits.

### Scaffold B: YouTube Short — POV + Screen-Record

```
[0-2s] FACELESS HOOK — text overlay: "POV: {role} + {situation} + {twist}"
[2-8s] PROBLEM REVEAL — voiceover (no face) over {Amazon listing / data table / VOC AI screen-recording}
[8-25s] METHOD — 3 quick cuts, each cut shows 1 specific number / 1 click in VOC AI
[25-35s] PAYOFF — single number on screen ("$340K opportunity" / "saved 3 hours" / etc)
[35-40s] PROFILE PROMPT — "follow for more {category} workflows" (subtle)
```

- Length: 35-45s max (TT/YT Shorts sweet spot)
- 1 number per cut (not multiple)
- NO link in description (link in bio only — same as Alinea)

### 05-video hook overlays

1. `POV: you sell on Amazon and your CMO just asked "why is BSR up but sales down"`
2. `{N} reviews. {N} complaints. {1} product opportunity nobody noticed.`
3. `Stop reading 1-star reviews wrong.`
4. `The {N}-second {workflow} that replaces your $2K/mo {vendor}.`
5. `If your listing has {trigger condition}, watch this.`

## Agent 02 — KOL / KOC

### Scaffold A: Outreach DM (cold)

```
{First-name} — {1-sentence specific reference to their recent content, ≤ 200 chars}.

We have {1 specific differentiator + 1 specific number} that would fit your {audience type} audience.

If you want to try it: {1-link to free tool, no signup}. Takes 30 seconds.

If not — fine. Either way, your {recent content reference} was {specific value attribute}.

— Hunter, voc.ai
```

- 4 paragraphs max
- 1 specific reference to their content (researched, not template-able by the agent — it's the Builder's job to surface the reference)
- No "I'd love to collaborate" / "do you accept paid partnerships" — those are auto-archive triggers
- Link goes to free tool, not pitch deck

### 02-kol-koc hook fill-ins (DM opener)

1. `Your {specific video/post} on {topic} from {timeframe} got it right about {finding}. {Related insight from VOC AI data}.`
2. `Saw your {content} on {topic}. We have {specific data} from {N} reviews that proves the opposite — happy to share if you want a follow-up angle.`
3. `{First name} — {specific reference}. Question: would your audience care about {specific insight}? We have the data.`
4. `Watching your {category} content for 3 months. The thing nobody's covered: {specific gap}. Want it?`
5. `{First name}, ignoring the obvious pitch — your {content piece} missed one thing: {specific finding}. Worth a follow-up?`

## Format Lock Enforcement

The Reviewer auto-rejects any draft that:

1. Does not match a scaffold above
2. Skips any required section (e.g. blog without "Worked example", Reddit without final question)
3. Mixes 2 scaffolds (e.g. Reddit Scaffold A's data forensics + Scaffold B's checklist — pick one)
4. Uses a hook that isn't one of the 5 fill-ins per agent (or a documented winning hook from `winning-hooks.md`)
5. Misses platform-specific hard rules (e.g. X "no I-start", LinkedIn "see more" earn)

**Templates evolve weekly.** When a winning post emerges that doesn't match a template, the Builder PROMOTES the format into this file. Anti-patterns go to `agents/<id>/anti-patterns.md`.
