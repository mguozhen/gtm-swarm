# TikTok — VOC AI Content Playbook

## Platform Rules

- **Format:** 30-60s vertical video, raw screen recordings or talking head
- **Posting frequency:** 3-5x/week
- **Best times:** 7-9pm EST weekdays, 11am-1pm weekends
- **Algorithm:** Watch-through rate is king. Hook in first 2 seconds. Shares > comments > likes

## Content Mix

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Live tool demo | 2x/week | "Watch me find a product gap in 30 seconds" |
| Shocking stat | 1x/week | One data point that makes sellers stop scrolling |
| "I found something" reveal | 1x/week | Show a real opportunity discovered in review data |
| Trend-jack / stitch | 1x/week | React to Amazon/e-commerce content with a data angle |
| Review horror story | 1x/2 weeks | Funny/painful real reviews that teach a lesson |

## Video Structure

```
[HOOK: 2 seconds]
Show the result FIRST. Text overlay + voiceover.
"I just found a $500K product opportunity in 30 seconds"

[CONTEXT: 5 seconds]
One sentence why this matters.
"Everyone uses keywords for product research. Nobody reads reviews properly."

[THE HOW: 30-40 seconds]
Screen recording of VOC AI. Show the actual analysis happening.
Point at specific reviews. Highlight the insight.

[RESULT: 5 seconds]
The payoff — the product gap, the sentiment pattern, the opportunity.
"Non-slip yoga mat with 6mm thickness. Only 3 products. $45 avg price."

[CTA: 3 seconds]
"Follow for more product research hacks" or "Free tool in bio"
```

## Hook Patterns

- "watch me find a product idea in 30 seconds" [screen recording starts]
- "your competitor's 1-star reviews are a goldmine. let me show you"
- "i analyzed 50,000 reviews and found something nobody's talking about"
- "stop using keywords for product research. do this instead"
- "the amazon product research method nobody teaches"
- "POV: you can read 10,000 reviews in 30 seconds"
- "this free tool just changed how i do product research"

## Content Formats

### 1. The Speed Analysis (highest performer)
Screen record opening VOC AI. Paste an ASIN. Show sentiment analysis loading. Point at the key insight. Done in 45 seconds.

### 2. The Review Roast
Show hilariously bad or surprisingly insightful real reviews. "This 1-star review just wrote a better product brief than most agencies." Educational + entertaining.

### 3. The Hidden Pattern
"Everyone looks at [obvious thing]. Nobody looks at [hidden thing]." Show a non-obvious insight from review data.

### 4. The Challenge
"Give me any Amazon product and I'll find a product opportunity in 60 seconds." Engage comments for ASINs.

### 5. The Stitch / Reaction
Stitch someone asking "how do I find products to sell on Amazon?" React with a live demo of review analysis.

## What NOT to Do

- No intros. Start with the hook IMMEDIATELY
- No stock footage or slides — show the real tool, real data
- No polished corporate videos
- No hard selling. Demonstrate the insight, not the tool
- No horizontal video. Vertical only
- Text overlays mandatory (80%+ watch without sound)

## Engagement Rules

- Reply to comments with video replies
- When someone comments an ASIN, make a follow-up video analyzing it
- Pin the most interesting product request → make a video answering it
- Duet Amazon seller content with a data angle

## Cross-Posting

- Every TikTok → YouTube Shorts (next day) → Instagram Reels (day after)
- Remove TikTok watermark before cross-posting
