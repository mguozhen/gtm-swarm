# Audience: Builders (Primary)

## Who They Are

Active Amazon sellers and e-commerce operators who are already doing product research and want a competitive edge. They KNOW reviews matter. They're spending hours reading them manually. They want a faster, smarter way.

## Demographics

- **Role:** Amazon seller (FBA/FBM), DTC brand owner, e-commerce product manager, sourcing lead
- **Business size:** Solo seller to 20-person team
- **Revenue:** $100K-$10M/year on Amazon
- **Selling experience:** 1-10+ years. Not beginners — they've launched products before
- **Tech comfort:** Medium-high. They use Helium 10, Jungle Scout, Keepa. Comfortable with Chrome extensions and dashboards
- **Age:** 25-50

## Their Pain Points

1. **Manual review reading is a time sink.** They spend 3-5 hours per product analyzing reviews. Multiply by 10 products they're researching = entire weeks lost
2. **Keyword tools all show the same data.** Everyone has Helium 10. Everyone sees the same "opportunities." Results: 47 sellers launching identical products
3. **Star ratings don't tell the story.** A 4.3 vs 4.4 tells you nothing about WHY. They need sentiment, not scores
4. **Competitor intel is guesswork.** They don't know what customers hate about competitor products — they're guessing based on their own assumptions
5. **Scaling research across markets.** US, UK, DE, JP — reading reviews in multiple languages is impossible manually

## What They Care About

- **Speed.** "How fast can I get an insight?" — 60-second analysis vs 3-hour manual read
- **Depth.** "Can it find what I can't find manually?" — patterns across 10,000 reviews, not 50
- **Accuracy.** "Will the sentiment analysis be right?" — trust in the AI's interpretation
- **Actionability.** "Can I make a sourcing decision based on this?" — not just charts, but answers
- **Cost.** "$99/mo is fine if it saves me 10 hours and helps me find a winning product"

## What Triggers Action

- Launched a product that flopped (didn't validate with review data)
- Watching a competitor grow in their niche and not understanding why
- Hitting a ceiling with keyword-only research tools
- Seeing a VOC AI demo that finds an insight in 30 seconds
- Free tool experience that delivers unexpected value

## Content That Converts Them

- Live demos showing a real ASIN analyzed in real-time
- "I found a product opportunity" stories with full methodology
- Competitor comparisons (VOC AI vs Helium 10 for review analysis)
- Specific category analyses they can relate to their niche
- Speed/ease content: "60 seconds, no login, paste an ASIN"

## Language They Use

- "product research," "niche analysis," "competitor reviews," "BSR"
- "sentiment," "customer feedback," "pain points," "product opportunity"
- "FBA," "private label," "sourcing," "listing optimization"
- They DON'T say: "voice of customer analytics," "NLP pipeline," "semantic analysis" — translate tech into seller outcomes
