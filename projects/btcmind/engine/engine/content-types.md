# Content Types — Format Definitions

## Primary Formats

### 1. Data Reveal
**Purpose:** Show a surprising insight from review analysis
**Structure:** "We analyzed X" → Key finding → What it means for sellers
**Best on:** X (data tweet), LinkedIn (narrative), Newsletter (deep-dive)
**Example:** "We analyzed 47,000 garlic press reviews. The #1 complaint isn't quality — it's handle durability."

### 2. Product Opportunity
**Purpose:** Show a real, actionable product gap found through review data
**Structure:** Category → Top product's weakness → Underserved niche → Opportunity
**Best on:** YouTube (full walkthrough), TikTok (speed demo), Newsletter
**Example:** "Non-slip yoga mat with 6mm thickness — only 3 of the top 50 have this"

### 3. Tool Demo / Tutorial
**Purpose:** Show VOC AI in action, teach a specific research workflow
**Structure:** Problem → Open tool → Live analysis → Insight → Takeaway
**Best on:** YouTube (main format), TikTok (30s speed version), X (video tweet)
**Example:** "How to find your competitor's biggest weakness in 60 seconds"

### 4. Competitor Teardown
**Purpose:** Analyze a real product/brand using review data
**Structure:** Pick product → Show reviews → Reveal hidden weakness → What a challenger should build
**Best on:** YouTube (SEO title), LinkedIn (strategy angle), X (thread)
**Example:** "The #1 wireless earbuds have a hidden problem — here's the data"

### 5. Contrarian / Hot Take
**Purpose:** Challenge conventional Amazon wisdom, spark debate
**Structure:** Bold claim → Evidence from data → "Change my mind"
**Best on:** X (single), Threads, LinkedIn (debate post)
**Example:** "Star ratings are useless. Sentiment is everything."

### 6. Seller Tip / Hack
**Purpose:** One actionable technique sellers can use today
**Structure:** The trick → Why it works → How to do it in 60 seconds
**Best on:** X (single/thread), Instagram (carousel), TikTok
**Example:** "Filter competitor reviews by 3-stars to find your product brief"

### 7. Review Story
**Purpose:** Use a real review (or pattern) to tell a larger business lesson
**Structure:** Show the review → What it reveals → The bigger principle
**Best on:** LinkedIn (narrative), X (screenshot tweet), TikTok (review roast)
**Example:** "This one 3-star review told a brand exactly why they were losing market share"

### 8. Industry / Category Analysis
**Purpose:** Macro-level data about an entire product category
**Structure:** "We looked at [category]" → Top trends → Opportunities → Threats
**Best on:** Newsletter (deep-dive), YouTube (data video), LinkedIn
**Example:** "What 2 billion reviews tell us about the home fitness market in 2026"

### 9. Community Discussion
**Purpose:** Start conversations, learn from sellers
**Structure:** Share observation → Ask genuine question
**Best on:** Facebook, LinkedIn, Threads
**Example:** "How do you use reviews in your product research process?"

### 10. Free Tool Spotlight
**Purpose:** Drive awareness of VOC AI's 20+ free tools
**Structure:** Problem → "Here's a free tool" → Quick demo → Result
**Best on:** TikTok (demo), X (video), YouTube Shorts
**Example:** "Free Amazon review analyzer. No login. Paste ASIN. Get insights."
