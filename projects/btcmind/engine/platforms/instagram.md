# Instagram — VOC AI Content Playbook

## Platform Rules

- **Format:** Carousel-first (7-10 slides), Reels secondary, Stories for engagement
- **Posting frequency:** 4-5x/week (3 carousels + 1 Reel + 1 Story series)
- **Best times:** 11am, 7pm EST weekdays; 10am weekends
- **Algorithm:** Saves and shares > likes. Carousel completion rate is #1 signal

## Content Mix

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Data reveal carousel | 2x/week | "We analyzed X reviews" — save-worthy insights |
| How-to carousel | 1x/week | Step-by-step product research guide |
| Reel (tool demo) | 1x/week | Cross-post from TikTok, slightly polished |
| Story series | 2-3x/week | Polls, Q&A, behind-the-scenes data |
| Quote / insight image | 1x/week | Bold data statement — shareable |

## Carousel Structure

```
Slide 1: THE HOOK — shocking data or bold claim (max 8 words)
         "Your Competitor's Reviews Are Telling You Everything"
Slide 2: THE CONTEXT — "Here's what we found..."
Slides 3-6: THE DATA — one insight per slide, clean visuals
Slide 7: THE TAKEAWAY — what to do with this information
Slide 8: CTA — "Save this" + "Follow @vocai for more"
```

## Tone

- Bold, clean, data-visual-first
- Short captions (under 150 words)
- Minimal emoji (1-2 max)
- "We" voice — brand as data authority
- Let the numbers be the hook, not adjectives

## Carousel Design Rules

- Consistent brand colors. Dark background + clean white/yellow text
- One chart, one number, or one review screenshot per slide
- Highlight key phrases in reviews with yellow/red markup
- Max 25 words per slide
- "Swipe →" cue on slide 1

## Example Carousel: "What 50,000 Reviews Reveal About Yoga Mats"

```
Slide 1: "We Read 50,000 Yoga Mat Reviews"
Slide 2: "#1 Complaint: 'Slippery when wet' (34% of 1-stars)"
Slide 3: "#1 Praise: 'Perfect thickness' (52% of 5-stars)"
Slide 4: "The Opportunity: Non-slip + 6mm thick = underserved"
Slide 5: "Only 3 products in top 50 combine both features"
Slide 6: "Average price of those 3: $45 (category avg: $28)"
Slide 7: "Premium positioning + solving the #1 pain = opportunity"
Slide 8: "Free review analyzer at voc.ai. Save this →"
```

## What NOT to Do

- No walls of text on slides
- No stock photos — use real review screenshots and data charts
- No inconsistent design across carousels
- No engagement bait ("like if you agree!")
- No hashtag spam (5-8 targeted hashtags max)

## Hashtag Strategy

- Broad: #amazonseller #ecommerce #productresearch
- Niche: #amazonreviews #sentimentanalysis #productopportunity #amazonFBA
- Rotate industry-specific: #yogamat #garlichpress #wirelessearbuds (match carousel topic)
