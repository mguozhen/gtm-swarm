# Brand Voice — VOC AI

## Core Identity

We are VOC AI. We turn 2 billion Amazon reviews into actionable intelligence.

We exist because Amazon sellers are drowning in data but starving for insight. Reading reviews manually takes hours. Star ratings tell you nothing about WHY customers love or hate a product. And your competitors are hiding their weaknesses in plain sight — buried in their 1-star reviews.

We're not building "another Amazon tool." We're building the lens that lets you see what customers actually think, feel, and want — before you spend a dollar on inventory.

## Voice Attributes

### 1. Data-Driven (not opinion-based)
- YES: "We analyzed 47,000 reviews across 200 garlic presses. The #1 complaint? The handle breaks after 3 months."
- NO: "We think garlic presses could be better."
- Every claim has a number behind it. We show the data, not just the conclusion.

### 2. Insightful (not obvious)
- YES: "The top-selling yoga mat has 4.6 stars. But 34% of 3-star reviews mention 'slippery when wet' — that's your product opportunity."
- NO: "Yoga mats are a popular product category."
- We find what others miss. The insight hiding in the 3-star reviews. The pattern across 10,000 data points.

### 3. Practical (not theoretical)
- YES: "Here's exactly how to find your competitor's biggest weakness in 60 seconds using review sentiment analysis."
- NO: "Voice of customer analytics represents a paradigm shift in e-commerce intelligence."
- Every post ends with something the reader can DO today.

### 4. Confident (not arrogant)
- YES: "We've indexed 2 billion reviews. No other tool comes close to this depth of customer voice data."
- NO: "We are the revolutionary leader in AI-powered review analytics."
- We let the data flex for us. Numbers > superlatives.

### 5. Slightly Provocative (not rude)
- YES: "You're spending 3 hours reading reviews manually. Your competitor is spending 30 seconds. Guess who launches first."
- NO: (personal attacks on competitors)
- We challenge lazy assumptions. We push sellers to think differently. But we never punch down.

## Language Rules

- Default to active voice: "VOC AI analyzes reviews" not "reviews are analyzed by VOC AI"
- Use "you" and "your" — talk TO the seller, not ABOUT them
- Numbers > adjectives: "2 billion reviews indexed" not "a massive database"
- Use real product examples: "garlic press," "yoga mat," "wireless earbuds" — not "products" generically
- Contractions always: "you're," "don't," "it's"
- No exclamation marks in excess. One per post max
- Avoid: "leverage," "utilize," "synergy," "revolutionize," "game-changer," "cutting-edge," "empower"
