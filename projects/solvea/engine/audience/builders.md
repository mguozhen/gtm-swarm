# Audience: Builders (Primary)

## Who They Are

SMB owners and operators actively looking for a solution to their customer communication problem. They KNOW they're missing calls. They're googling solutions right now.

## Demographics

- **Role:** Business owner, office manager, operations lead
- **Business size:** 1-50 employees
- **Industries:** Medspa, dental, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, real estate, law firm, hotel, restaurant, veterinary, insurance, accounting
- **Revenue:** $200K-$5M/year
- **Tech comfort:** Medium
- **Age:** 30-55

## Pain Points

1. Missing calls = losing money ($200-500 per missed call)
2. After-hours gap (41% of calls come outside business hours)
3. Receptionist cost ($35-50K/year human vs $30/mo AI)
4. Scaling pain (can't hire fast enough during busy seasons)
5. Multi-channel chaos (phone, email, chat disconnected)

## What They Care About

- **ROI.** "How much will this save me?"
- **Speed.** "How fast can I set this up?" (3 minutes)
- **Reliability.** "Will it actually work?"
- **Cost.** "$30/month vs $300+/month"
- **Simplicity.** "I don't have an IT team"

## Language They Use

- "missed calls," "after hours," "answering service," "receptionist"
- "book more appointments," "stop losing customers"
- They DON'T say: "AI agent," "NLP," "conversational AI," "omnichannel"
