# Instagram — Solvea Content Playbook

## Platform Rules

- **Format:** Carousel-first (7-10 slides), Reels secondary, Stories for engagement
- **Posting frequency:** 4-5x/week (3 carousels + 1 Reel + 1 Story series)
- **Best times:** 11am, 7pm EST
- **Algorithm:** Saves and shares > likes. Carousel completion rate is #1 signal

## Content Mix

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Educational carousel | 2x/week | Step-by-step guides, tips — save-worthy |
| Before/after carousel | 1x/week | Customer results with numbers |
| Reel (demo or stat) | 1x/week | Cross-post from TikTok |
| Story series | 2-3x/week | Polls, Q&A, behind-the-scenes |
| Quote / single image | 1x/week | Bold statement — shareable |

## Carousel Structure

```
Slide 1: Bold claim or shocking stat (max 8 words)
Slides 2-6: Steps, data, story progression (one point per slide)
Slide 7: Summary or key takeaway
Slide 8: CTA — "Save this" + "Follow @solvea"
```

## Example: "5 Calls You're Missing Every Day"

```
Slide 1: "5 Calls You're Missing Every Day"
Slide 2: "The 6:30pm Call" — highest intent, you're closed
Slide 3: "The Lunch Rush Call" — staff too busy
Slide 4: "The Weekend Call" — they wanted to book Monday
Slide 5: "The Hold-Too-Long Call" — hung up after 45s
Slide 6: "The After-Voicemail Call" — called competitor instead
Slide 7: "Each one = $200-500 in lost revenue"
Slide 8: "The fix takes 3 minutes. Follow @solvea →"
```

## What NOT to Do

- No walls of text on slides (max 25 words)
- No stock photos — use real screenshots and data
- No engagement bait
- 5-8 targeted hashtags max
