# LinkedIn — Solvea Content Playbook

## Platform Rules

- **Character limit:** 3,000 per post (aim for 1,200-1,800 for optimal engagement)
- **Posting frequency:** 3-4x/week
- **Best posting times:** Tue-Thu 8-10am local time of your audience
- **Algorithm priority:** dwell time, comments > reactions

## Content Mix

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Founder story / lesson | 1x/week | Build trust, show the human behind the product |
| Customer ROI breakdown | 1x/week | Social proof with hard numbers |
| Industry insight | 1x/week | Position as thought leader in AI + SMB space |
| Contrarian / debate post | 1x/2 weeks | Spark comments, algorithm fuel |

## Tone on LinkedIn

- Professional but warm. "Smart friend who runs a successful company"
- First person ("I") — personal stories outperform brand announcements 10:1
- Paragraphs of 1-2 sentences max
- Story-data-story sandwich structure

## Hook Patterns

- "Last month, I watched a [industry] owner nearly cry on a call with us. Here's why:"
- "We analyzed [X] customer calls. The #1 reason people hang up isn't what you think."
- "A $30/month tool just outperformed a $5,000/month answering service. Here's the data:"
- "The hardest conversation I have with business owners isn't about AI. It's about the calls they never knew they missed."

## Example Post

```
Last week a dentist in Ohio told me something that stuck with me.

"I didn't realize I was losing patients until I saw the numbers."

He'd been running his practice for 12 years. Good reviews. Full schedule most days.

But when we looked at his call data:
→ 34% of calls went to voicemail
→ Average callback time: 4.7 hours  
→ 68% of voicemail callers never picked up the callback

He wasn't losing patients because of bad dentistry.
He was losing them because nobody answered the phone.

We set up an AI receptionist for his practice. Took about 3 minutes.

First month results:
- 97% of calls answered on first ring
- 23 new patient bookings that would have been voicemails
- $18,400 in recovered revenue

The wildest part? He said "I had no idea this was happening."

That's the thing about missed calls. They're invisible losses.

Do you actually know how many calls you're missing right now?
```

## What NOT to Do

- No "I'm excited to share" / "Thrilled to announce"
- No posting links in main post (put in first comment)
- No emoji spam. Let the words work
