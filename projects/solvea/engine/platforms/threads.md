# Threads — Solvea Content Playbook

## Platform Rules

- **Format:** Text-first, 500 chars max, chain up to 10
- **Posting frequency:** 3-5x/week
- **Best times:** 9-11pm EST, 7-8am
- **Algorithm:** Replies and conversation depth

## Tone

- Late-night thought energy. Sharp observations, casual delivery
- Lowercase always. Even more relaxed than X
- NO selling. NO links. NO CTAs. Just be interesting

## Hook Patterns

- "kinda wild that most businesses are unreachable for 58% of the day"
- "nobody talks about how voicemail is basically a 'go away' button"
- "unpopular opinion: hiring a receptionist in 2026 is like hiring someone to send faxes"
- "the most expensive sound in business is a phone ringing in an empty room"

## Example Posts

**Hot take:**
```
kinda wild that most service businesses spend $5K/month on google ads

then send 34% of those leads to voicemail

that's like paying for a packed restaurant and locking the front door
```

**Observation:**
```
noticed something weird

every business owner i talk to thinks they miss "maybe 2-3 calls a day"

then they check the actual data and it's like 8-12

missed calls are the most invisible money leak in any business
```

**Chain:**
```
Post 1: the answering service industry is a $5 billion industry built on one assumption: you need humans to answer phones

Post 2: but an AI can answer in 0.5 seconds, in 12 languages, 24/7, never call in sick. for $30/month

Post 3: not saying human receptionists are bad. just saying paying $300-800/month for what costs $30 is a pricing problem, not a quality one
```

## What NOT to Do

- No links, no hashtags, no product pitches
