# X (Twitter) — Solvea Content Playbook

## Platform Rules

- **Character limit:** 280 per tweet, threads up to 25 tweets
- **Posting frequency:** 5-7x/week minimum (mix of singles + threads)
- **Best posting times:** 8am, 12pm, 5pm EST (weekdays); 10am weekends
- **Algorithm priority:** replies, bookmarks, and retweets > likes. Threads that get early engagement get pushed hard

## Content Mix

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Pain point tweets | 3x/week | Stop the scroll, make them feel seen |
| Mini case studies | 2x/week | Proof that Solvea works (real numbers) |
| Hot takes | 1x/week | Contrarian opinions that spark replies |
| Threads (5-12 tweets) | 1x/week | Deep value, system breakdowns |
| Product demos (video) | 1x/week | Show, don't tell |

## Tone on X

- Lowercase casual. no caps unless emphasizing ONE word
- Short sentences. punch hard
- Talk like a business owner texting a friend, not a brand posting content
- Use "you" — make it personal
- Numbers stop the scroll: "$4,200/month in missed calls" hits harder than "a lot of money"
- Line breaks between every thought. white space = readability on mobile

## Hook Patterns That Work on X

- "you're losing $__/month and don't even know it"
- "i watched a [industry] owner miss 23 calls in one week. here's what happened next:"
- "hot take: [conventional wisdom] is dead. here's what's replacing it"
- "the difference between a $50K/year business and a $500K/year business? they answer the phone"
- "3 minutes. that's how long it takes to never miss a customer call again"
- "[competitor] charges $300/month. here's what $30/month gets you:"

## Example Posts

**Pain point single:**
```
you didn't start your business to spend 3 hours a day returning voicemails

but here you are. every night. listening to "hi, I was calling about..."

there's a reason your competitor books more clients. they answer the phone. every time.
```

**Mini case study:**
```
a medspa in miami was missing 40% of after-hours calls

each missed call = ~$350 in lost botox/filler revenue

they set up solvea in 3 minutes

30 days later:
- 97% calls answered
- $14K in recovered revenue
- zero additional staff

the math isn't complicated
```

**Hot take:**
```
hot take: voicemail is the worst customer experience invention ever created

nobody leaves voicemails anymore. they just call your competitor

the businesses winning in 2026 don't have "better voicemail greetings"

they have AI that picks up on the first ring. 24/7. in any language
```

## What NOT to Do

- No hashtags (they look spammy on X in 2026)
- No "excited to announce" corporate speak
- No threads that are just a blog post chopped into tweets
- Don't start with "I" — start with "you" or a hook
- No links in tweet 1 of a thread (kills reach). Put link in last tweet or reply
