# YouTube — Solvea Content Playbook

## Platform Rules

- **Video length:** 8-12 minutes
- **Posting frequency:** 1x/week
- **Upload time:** Thursday or Friday 2-4pm EST
- **Algorithm priority:** CTR (thumbnail + title) > watch time > engagement

## Content Mix

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Setup tutorial | 2x/month | SEO magnet, onboarding tool |
| Industry deep-dive | 2x/month | "AI receptionist for [industry]" — long-tail SEO |
| Competitor comparison | 1x/month | Capture decision-stage searchers |
| Customer story / results | 1x/month | Social proof in video format |
| Live demo / stress test | 1x/month | "Watch AI handle 5 real calls" — curiosity driver |

## Video Structure (8-12 min)

```
[HOOK — 0:00-0:30]
State the problem + promise the payoff
"If you run a [industry] and you're still relying on voicemail, 
you're leaving money on the table."

[CONTEXT — 0:30-2:00]
Why this matters. The cost of missed calls. Quick stats.

[MAIN CONTENT — 2:00-9:00]
Step-by-step walkthrough with screen recording:
- The problem (show the pain with real data)
- The solution (live demo of Solvea setup)
- Configuration tips (knowledge base, integrations)
- Results (show real metrics)
- Pro tips (advanced features)

[RECAP + CTA — 9:00-10:00]
"Link in description to try free — handles your first 50 
customers, no credit card."
```

## Title Formulas

- "How to Set Up an AI Receptionist in 3 Minutes (Live Demo)"
- "AI Receptionist vs Human Receptionist: I Tested Both for 30 Days"
- "Best AI Receptionist for [Industry] in 2026 (Honest Review)"
- "I Replaced My $3,000/Month Answering Service with a $30 AI"
- "The Hidden Cost of Missed Calls (And How to Fix It in 3 Minutes)"

## YouTube Shorts

- 30-60s clips from main videos
- "Watch this AI answer a real customer call"
- Post 2-3 Shorts per main video
