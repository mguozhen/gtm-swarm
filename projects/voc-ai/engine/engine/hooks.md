# Hooks — Opening Line Formulas

Hooks decide 80% of content performance. For VOC AI, our hooks are powered by data — surprising numbers, hidden patterns, and contrarian insights from 2 billion reviews.

## Hook Categories

### 1. Data Bomb
A specific number from review analysis that makes them stop.

- "we analyzed 47,000 reviews across 200 garlic presses. the #1 reason for returns? not what you think"
- "34% of 3-star reviews contain a specific product feature request. almost nobody reads them"
- "2 billion reviews. 500 categories. one pattern shows up everywhere"
- "the #1 complaint in wireless earbuds isn't sound quality. it's the charging case lid"
- "we tracked 50 million keywords. but the real signal was hiding in the review text"

### 2. Competitor Intelligence
Turn competitor weaknesses into your content hook.

- "your competitor's 1-star reviews are a goldmine. here's what's inside"
- "the bestselling product in your niche has a fatal flaw. customers are screaming about it"
- "i read your competitor's last 2,000 reviews. here's what they're getting wrong"
- "the fastest-growing product in [category] didn't invent anything new. they just fixed the #1 complaint"

### 3. Contrarian / Hot Take
Challenge how sellers think about product research.

- "hot take: star ratings are the most useless metric in e-commerce"
- "unpopular opinion: keyword research alone will get you crushed in 2026"
- "stop looking at best sellers rank. start reading 3-star reviews"
- "everyone's using the same keyword tools finding the same 'opportunities.' that's why 47 sellers launch the same product"
- "you don't need another keyword tool. you need to listen to customers"

### 4. Curiosity Gap
Open a loop they need to close.

- "i found a $500K product opportunity in 60 seconds. it was hiding in plain sight"
- "there's a reason one brand is growing 3x faster than competitors in the same niche. it's not their price"
- "we showed this data to a 7-figure seller. his exact words: 'how did I miss this?'"
- "the most valuable review on any Amazon listing isn't the 1-star or the 5-star"

### 5. Direct Challenge
Call out the seller directly.

- "you're spending 3 hours reading reviews manually. your competitor is spending 30 seconds"
- "quick question: do you actually know what customers hate about your product?"
- "you spent $10K on inventory for a product nobody asked for. reviews could have told you"
- "how many hours did you spend on product research last month? now how many of those hours were reading actual customer feedback?"

### 6. Result-First
Lead with the outcome.

- "one ASIN. 30 seconds of analysis. a product gap worth $340K/year. here's the breakdown:"
- "$500K in first-year revenue. the product idea came from a 3-star review. here's how:"
- "from 47,000 reviews to 1 clear product opportunity. the entire process took 60 seconds"

### 7. Speed / Ease
Emphasize how fast review intelligence works.

- "60 seconds. that's how long it takes to analyze 10,000 reviews"
- "i used to spend 3 hours reading reviews. now i spend 30 seconds. same insights. zero pain"
- "free tool. no login. paste an ASIN. get the answer. that's it"
- "20 free tools. no signup. no credit card. just data"

## Platform Adaptation

| Hook Type | X | LinkedIn | YouTube | Instagram | TikTok |
|-----------|---|----------|---------|-----------|--------|
| Data Bomb | lead tweet | line 1-2 | first 5 sec | slide 1 | text overlay |
| Competitor Intel | thread opener | story setup | provocative title | "swipe to see" | "look at this" |
| Contrarian | standalone tweet | debate starter | SEO title | bold slide 1 | stitch hook |
| Curiosity Gap | "thread 🧵" | earn "see more" | "watch what I found" | "swipe →" | "wait for it" |
| Direct Challenge | reply-bait | question at end | look at camera | poll sticker | POV format |
| Result-First | thread opener | buried after story | title + thumbnail | slide 1 teaser | show result first |
| Speed / Ease | standalone | CTA-adjacent | demo title | CTA slide | speed recording |

## Rules

- Always include at least ONE specific number in the hook
- Never start with "I" on X (start with data or "you")
- LinkedIn hooks must earn the "see more" click
- YouTube hooks must work in 5 seconds with zero context
- Track which hooks get highest bookmark/save rate
- Update this file weekly with hooks that performed well
