## Content Production Batch

**Monday:** Pick weekly topic, draft X thread + LinkedIn
**Tuesday:** Finalize X + LinkedIn, record YouTube
**Wednesday:** Edit YouTube, draft IG carousel, draft remaining platforms
**Thursday:** Upload YouTube, send newsletter, schedule remaining
**Friday-Sunday:** Engage, note what performed, capture ideas for next week

## Rules

- Never publish all platforms same day
- YouTube Thursday, everything orbits around it
- X gets most posts (5-7/week) — low effort, high signal
- Quality > quantity: skip a post before publishing mediocre content
- Reactive content (Amazon news, trending products) can break the schedule
