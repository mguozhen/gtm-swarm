# YouTube — VOC AI Content Playbook

## Platform Rules

- **Video length:** 8-12 minutes
- **Posting frequency:** 1x/week
- **Upload time:** Thursday or Friday 2-4pm EST
- **Algorithm priority:** CTR (thumbnail + title) > watch time > engagement

## Content Mix

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|------|-----------|---------|
| Product research walkthrough | 2x/month | "I analyzed 10,000 reviews for [category]" — SEO magnet |
| Tool tutorial / demo | 2x/month | How to use VOC AI for specific research tasks |
| Competitor comparison | 1x/month | "VOC AI vs Helium 10: which finds better insights?" |
| "I found a product opportunity" | 1x/month | Full research process from review analysis to product brief |
| Data deep-dive | 1x/month | "What 2 billion reviews tell us about [category/trend]" |

## Video Structure (8-12 min)

```
[HOOK — 0:00-0:30]
"I analyzed 10,000 reviews in the [category] niche and found 
a product gap nobody's exploiting. By the end of this video 
you'll know exactly what it is and how to find opportunities 
like this yourself."

[CONTEXT — 0:30-2:00]
Why reviews matter more than keywords for product research.
Quick setup of the problem or opportunity.

[MAIN CONTENT — 2:00-9:00]
Live screen recording walkthrough:
- Section 1: Finding the niche / ASIN (show the process)
- Section 2: Running review analysis (live in VOC AI)
- Section 3: Reading the sentiment data (what jumps out)
- Section 4: The insight (the product gap or opportunity)
- Section 5: How to validate it (cross-reference with sales data)

[RECAP + CTA — 9:00-10:00]
Summarize the finding. Show the potential.
CTA: "Free tools at voc.ai — no login needed. Try the review 
analyzer on your own niche. Subscribe for weekly product research."
```

## Title Formulas

- "I Analyzed 10,000 Amazon Reviews and Found a $500K Product Gap"
- "How to Find Winning Products Using Customer Reviews (Not Keywords)"
- "VOC AI vs Helium 10: Which Tool Actually Finds Better Products?"
- "What 2 Billion Amazon Reviews Reveal About [Category]"
- "The Review Analysis Method That Found My Last 3 Winning Products"
- "Your Competitor's 1-Star Reviews Are a Goldmine (Here's How to Mine Them)"
- "I Found a Product Opportunity in 60 Seconds (Free Tool)"

## Thumbnail Rules

- Face + emotion (surprised, pointing at screen) OR bold text + data visual
- Max 4-5 words on thumbnail
- Show a review screenshot or data chart in the background
- Before/after split: "Reading Reviews Manually" vs "$500K Product Idea"
- Use red/yellow highlights on review text in thumbnails

## What NOT to Do

- No intros longer than 30 seconds
- No generic "how to sell on Amazon" without a unique data angle
- No clickbait that doesn't deliver
- No talking head without screen recordings (show the tool)
- Don't forget timestamps in description

## YouTube Shorts

- 30-60s clips from main videos
- "Watch me find a product opportunity in 30 seconds"
- Screen recording of review analysis with text overlay
- Post 2-3 Shorts per main video
